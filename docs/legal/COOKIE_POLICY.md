## 1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Marek Čermák ("we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on the Remanence website and dashboard (the "Website"). It should be read alongside our [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY_POLICY.md).

This policy does not apply to the Remanence API (Cloud Service), which does not use cookies. API authentication is handled via Bearer tokens in HTTP headers.

## 2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website. They are widely used to make websites work, improve user experience, and provide information to site operators. Similar technologies include local storage, session storage, and pixel tags.

## 3. Cookies We Use

### 3.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the Website to function and cannot be disabled.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration | Type |
|--------|---------|----------|------|
| `session_id` | Maintains your authenticated session on the dashboard | Session (expires on browser close) | First-party |
| `csrf_token` | Protects against cross-site request forgery attacks | Session | First-party |

**Legal basis:** Legitimate interest — these cookies are necessary for the Website to function securely. No consent is required.

### 3.2 Analytics Cookies (Opt-in)

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with the Website. They are **only set after you provide explicit consent** via our cookie banner.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration | Type |
|--------|---------|----------|------|
| Analytics provider cookies (TBD) | Page views, navigation patterns, performance metrics | Up to 12 months | Third-party or first-party (depends on provider) |

We will update this table with specific cookie names once an analytics provider is selected. We prioritize privacy-respecting analytics solutions (e.g., Plausible, Fathom, Umami) that do not track users across websites.

**Legal basis:** Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR) — set only after explicit opt-in.

### 3.3 Cookies We Do NOT Use

We do not use:
- **Advertising cookies** — we do not serve ads or participate in ad networks.
- **Third-party tracking cookies** — we do not allow third parties to track you across websites.
- **Social media cookies** — we do not embed social media widgets that set tracking cookies.

## 4. Cookie Consent

When you first visit the Website, a cookie consent banner will appear allowing you to:
- **Accept all** — enables strictly necessary + analytics cookies.
- **Reject non-essential** — enables only strictly necessary cookies.
- **Manage preferences** — choose which categories to enable or disable.

You can change your cookie preferences at any time via the cookie settings link in the Website footer.

## 5. How to Control Cookies

In addition to our consent banner, you can control cookies through your browser settings:

- **Chrome:** Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
- **Firefox:** Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari:** Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and Website Data
- **Edge:** Settings → Privacy, Search, and Services → Cookies

Note: Blocking strictly necessary cookies may prevent the Website dashboard from functioning correctly.

We may update this Cookie Policy when we add or remove cookies, or when required by law. Material changes will be communicated via the Website. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates the most recent revision.
